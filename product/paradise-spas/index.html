    • About This Spa

    The Artesian Grand Bahama is the flagship of the Island Spas line — built for families who want the ultimate backyard hydrotherapy experience. With 44 to 64 Helix jets, a full-length lounger, and Artesian's patented DirectFlow Personal Control, every seat delivers a customized massage. Engineered for cold climates and built to last, the Grand Bahama is backed by Artesian's industry-leading warranty and Paradise Spas' lifetime service commitment.

    Key Features

    Patented Helix Jets

    Artesian's patented Helix jets deliver a spiraling, double-helix water massage that targets muscles with precision. Available in 44 to 64 jet configurations.

    DirectFlow Personal Control

    Every seat has its own flow control valve so each bather can adjust water pressure independently — no fighting over jets.

    Full Foam Insulation

    Artesian's full foam insulation keeps heat in and energy costs down — critical for North Dakota winters. Designed to perform in extreme cold.

    Cascade Falls

    The built-in Cascade Falls waterfall adds a soothing ambiance and neck massage option, turning your spa into a true backyard retreat.
